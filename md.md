Please ensure you request an invitation **before submitting** your entry.

- **$10,000 USD** to the winner, paid within **30 days** of verification.

- Round-trip economy airfare to **Los Angeles**
- **2–3 nights** of standard hotel lodging

## Submission Requirements

- Original, **unproduced** scripts only
- Entrant must affirm **sole authorship** or co-writer consent
- **Length Guidelines:**
  - Shorts: _5–30 pages_
  - TV Pilots: _30–65 pages_
  - Features: _85–130 pages_
- Scripts must be in **English** and standard **industry format**
- **Illegible or incomplete** entries may be disqualified

## Judging & Selection

Blind judging by **producers**, **professional screenwriters**, and **development executives**.

| Round            | Date            |
| ---------------- | --------------- |
| Quarterfinalists | July 10, 2026   |
| Semifinalists    | July 20, 2026   |
| Finalists        | August 3, 2026  |
| Winner           | August 14, 2026 |

**Judging Criteria:**

- Storytelling & Originality — **30%**
- Characterization & Emotional Stakes — **25%**
- Craft (structure, pacing, formatting, dialogue) — **25%**
- Market Potential — **20%**

In case of a tie, higher scores in **Storytelling**, then **Characterization**, then **Craft** determine the winner.  
All judges’ decisions are **final**.
